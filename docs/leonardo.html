The CINECA Super-Leonardo, brought online in 1998, was a cutting-edge supercomputer located in Italy, dedicated to academic and research institutions for extensive scientific research.

This powerful system was used for a wide range of scientific applications, from climate simulations and biochemical modeling to quantum physics calculations.

With its impressive processing capabilities, Super-Leonardo played a crucial role in advancing Italy's position in the international scientific community, providing researchers with a valuable tool for conducting sophisticated, large-scale computations.
